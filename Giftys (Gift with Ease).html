Our Philosophy

My great-great grandmother starting making gift baskets for her loved ones at the age of 15. She just wanted a simple, fast, and easy way to show people she loved and cared about them. Making a gift basket with a personally selected assortment of items also meant creativity and thoughfulness went in to every gift. She passed this tradition down to her daughter whom in turn, passed it down to her daughter(my grandmother). My grandmother loved this tradition so much, she decided to share it with the world. Giftys was born! Since long before this company was passed to me, our tradition has had one central philosophy. To make giving thoughtful and meaningful gifts easy! Giftys, giving gifts with ease!
